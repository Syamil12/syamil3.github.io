Peyeum

Peyeum is like tape but has a drier texture. If the tape usually contains a lot of water which tastes sweet and a little sour, then peyeum is the opposite even though it is made from the same ingredients, namely cassava.

Bandros

Bandros is wheat flour mixed with coconut milk. Then, the dough is cooked on a concave mold. After the bandros are cooked, they are served with a sprinkling of grated coconut as an extra.


Tofu Milk Lembang

Like most tofu, tofu milk lembang is made with soy-based ingredients. But the presence of milk in the dough makes this one food have a softer and more savory taste.
